Send Waste

Help us recycle more by sending us your waste plastics.

Step 1

Separate your waste into each type of material.

Types of waste can include

  • Unwanted Prints
  • Failed Prints
  • Colour changing towers/purges
  • Leftover or unwanted filament

Plastics Accepted:

  • PLA
  • PETG
  • More will be added soon

Step 2

Package your waste securely and print a prepaid shipping label.

Step 3

Drop it off at your nearest collection point. We'll handle the recycling.
